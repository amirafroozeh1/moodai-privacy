Privacy Policy

MoodAI Tracker - Last updated: 01/01/2026

Your privacy is extremely important to us. MoodAI Tracker is designed with a strict privacy-first approach. Your data belongs to you, stays under your control, and is never sold, shared, or transmitted.

1. Personal Data

MoodAI Tracker does not require user accounts and does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to any backend servers.

Any information you choose to enter remains only on your device, including:

  • Name or nickname (if entered)
  • Email address (if entered)
  • Mood entries, journal notes, memories
  • Any text or content you type within the app

Your entries and data are fully private and entirely under your control.

MoodAI Tracker is designed and developed in Amsterdam, Netherlands.

AI-powered features in MoodAI Tracker are built with privacy in mind. Your content is never uploaded to external servers, no personal data is used to train AI models, and AI processing does not store or share identifiable data.

MoodAI Tracker does not use analytics services, crash reporting tools, advertising networks, or third-party SDKs that collect data. This means no tracking, no profiling, and no data sharing.

6. Cookies

MoodAI Tracker does not use cookies for tracking purposes. The app does not collect any data from cookies to the cloud, and no cookie data is transmitted to external servers. We prioritize your privacy and do not track user activity.
